How To Edit A Feature Film By Yourself!

You will learn what it takes to edit a great film by yourself, on your own, using what you have at your disposal. Editing is all about storytelling. And having a good understanding is crucial to be able to finish your film and be able to bring it to the audience. Learn how to edit, grade, work with sound all by yourself and how to make your film look and sound better. We will dive into tips and techniques that will help you improve your editing and give you an idea of what it takes to bring film from raw footage to final master. For years I’ve made independent films and had to make a lot of mistakes. Learn from mine. Let's take that footage and bring it to life. Happy filmmaking.
